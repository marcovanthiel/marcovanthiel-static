Alongside my work in IT and consultancy I collect art. A growing personal archive of contemporary works — painting, photography, sculpture — with attention to the makers and their stories.

## A custom archive

The collection is managed in a custom web application: an editorial magazine that records works, artists and provenance. After a short request, visitors gain access to a filtered version that shows artworks and biographies — financial details, locations and internal notes stay private.

## Who is it for?

Anyone interested — fellow collectors, art professionals, students or curious visitors — can request access. Every request is reviewed personally; once approved, you'll receive an email confirming you can log in.
