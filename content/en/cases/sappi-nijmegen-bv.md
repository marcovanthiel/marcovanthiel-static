Sappi Nijmegen BV — IT Carve-out and Audit

In the context of a planned acquisition, a carve-out plan was drawn up for the entire IT organization of Sappi Nijmegen. The audit covered infrastructure, MES, ERP, office automation, telephony and support processes, and delivered a complete IT dossier for due diligence.
