Royal Smit BV — Chief Information Officer (2016–2017)

CIO role at an international transformer manufacturer with 700 employees and € 300 million in revenue. Development and delivery of a five-year vision and strategy for the international business unit. Leadership of a 15-person department in an engineer-to-order production environment. Strategic selection and implementation of enterprise systems supporting the production process, including business-case development for a production-optimization program.
