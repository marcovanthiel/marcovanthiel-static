InnovioPapers BV — Chief Information Officer during Carve-out and Transition (2014–2016)

CIO and strategic program manager during the spin-off of paper mill InnovioPapers (300 employees, € 100 million in revenue) from the South African multinational Sappi Paper. Integrated steering of the complete carve-out of the ICT organization, application landscape and infrastructure. Implementation of a Manufacturing Execution System (MES) and infrastructure optimization. Redesign of supplier management for continuity and scalability.
