# Senior Program Manager for complex organizational transitions | Supervisory Board Member | Interim Executive & CIO

Senior manager, program manager and supervisory board member with more than 20 years of experience delivering complex organizational transitions and multi-year change programs in education, healthcare and industry. The track record of executive and program steering extends to organizations with € 1.4 billion in revenue and more than 13,000 employees, including mergers, carve-outs, restructurings and strategic transformations — among others as CIO of two international industrial organizations.

The strength lies in structuring complex issues, critically testing strategic choices and finding practical solutions — even in adversity. Strategic insight and operational excellence are combined in leading multidisciplinary teams, managing executive and stakeholder relationships and steering toward delivery of organizational goals. Working methodologies: Prince2, MSP, Lean and transition management.

With the supervisory board program at Maastricht University, an ongoing MBA Leading Innovation and Change and concrete governance experience — as chair of a secondary-school supervisory board and member of a housing-corporation supervisory board — both governance and operational expertise are brought to weightier executive and supervisory roles.

## Core Competencies

* **Program management** — integrated steering of complex, multi-year transformation programs
* **Organizational change** — mergers, carve-outs, restructurings, process redesign and cultural change
* **Strategy and executive steering** — development of multi-year visions, roadmaps and business cases in coordination with the executive board and supervisory board
* **Stakeholder management** — collaboration with executive boards, directors, financiers, works councils and external partners
* **Team leadership** — leading multidisciplinary teams and project leaders, coaching during transitions
* **Vendor management** — leading complex European tenders and supplier relationships
* **Governance and supervision** — executive and supervisory experience in education, healthcare, housing corporations and culture
* **Domain knowledge of digital transformation** — pragmatic deployment of information and systems issues as part of the broader change program

## Availability

Immediately available for interim assignments as senior program manager, interim executive, strategic advisor, CIO or change manager within organizations working on mergers, restructurings and broader organizational transitions. Also available for supervisory roles in healthcare, education, housing corporations and cultural institutions.
