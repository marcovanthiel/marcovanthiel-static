# Privacy statement

This website processes as little personal data as possible. Below is a clear summary of what is and is not done, in line with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## Controller

Marco van Thiel — Van Thiel Management & Consultancy
Nijmegen, the Netherlands
E-mail: [marco@marcovanthiel.nl](mailto:marco@marcovanthiel.nl)

## What data

- **Visitor analytics**: this site uses **no analytics cookies** (such as Google Analytics) and no tracking pixels. Cloudflare logs IP addresses at the network level for security purposes (DDoS protection); those logs are managed under Cloudflare's own privacy statement.
- **Contact forms**: there is no contact form on this site. Communication runs via e-mail or phone. Messages you send me are retained as long as relevant to the working relationship.
- **Cookies**: this site sets no tracking cookies. Cloudflare may set a functional cookie for security; that is exempt from consent requirements.

## Legal basis

The basis for any processing is "performance of a (potential) contract" or "legitimate interest" (website security).

## Your rights

Under GDPR you may request access, correction, or deletion of your data. Send an e-mail to [marco@marcovanthiel.nl](mailto:marco@marcovanthiel.nl).

## Complaint

If you believe your data is being processed incorrectly, you can lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority via [autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl](https://www.autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl/).
