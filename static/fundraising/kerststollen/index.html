A stollen that makes a difference

A Christmas stollen is a classic Dutch gift. For the neighbour, for the office, for guests dropping by on Boxing Day. We're turning it into a present that quietly does something far bigger.

The full proceeds — minus the wholesale price from our baker — go to the All4small foundation. The money funds research that helps neonatal intensive-care doctors monitor newborns' circulation more safely. No pain, no discomfort, but better outcomes for the smallest patients in the country.

Kiwanis Cuijk has been raising tens of thousands of euros for All4small with similar drives for years. This year for the first time together with Rotary Nijmegen Stad en Land — two service clubs, one mission.

"Every slice of a stollen is a treat — and every stollen is one step closer to better care for the children who can't yet speak up for themselves."

— Kiwanis Cuijk, 2025 Christmas drive

About the All4small foundation

All4small was founded on the initiative of paediatricians, neonatologists and nurses at Radboudumc. Their goal: to help these fragile, tiny patients even better, and to draw attention to research that increases their chances of survival.

Research that matters

Concretely, All4small contributes to developing a precise, pain-free method for monitoring newborns' blood circulation — usable without the baby noticing a thing.

Couveuse Challenge

Through, among other things, the Couveuse Challenge, All4small has built bridges to four hospitals across the country. Donations from this drive extend that network further.

Onward, for the tiniest

Since its founding, All4small has funded tens of thousands of euros' worth of research — thanks to service clubs, companies and individuals like you.
